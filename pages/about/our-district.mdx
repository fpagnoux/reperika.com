The city of Somerville is a diverse community with over [fifty languages spoken](https://www.somervillema.gov/about) other than English including Spanish, Portuguese, Chinese, and Hatian Creole. The city is [comprised of 66.4% renters](https://www.towncharts.com/Massachusetts/Housing/Somerville-city-MA-Housing-data.html#:~:text=Somerville%20depicts%20it%20has%20a%20Renter%20Percent%20of%2066.4%25%20which,places%20in%20the%20local%20area) with an [average age is 30.4 years old](https://censusreporter.org/profiles/62000US25144-27th-middlesex-district-ma/).
