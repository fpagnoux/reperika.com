## [Joint Committee on Elder Affairs](https://malegislature.gov/Committees/Detail/J44/192/Members)

The Joint Committee on Elder Affairs considers matters concerning the elderly, seniors with disabilities, nursing facilities, prescription drugs, reverse mortgages, and senior pharmacy matters.

House Chair: Rep. [Thomas M. Stanley](https://malegislature.gov/Legislators/Profile/TMS1/192/Members)

House Vice Chair: Rep. [Kate Lipper-Garabedian](https://malegislature.gov/Legislators/Profile/KLG1/192/Members)

## [Joint Committee on Export Development](https://malegislature.gov/Committees/Detail/J51/192/Members)

The Joint Committee on Export Development considers matters concerning the Commonwealth's exports, trade, and international competitiveness; cultivation and promotion of Massachusetts as a global economic leader; development of partnerships between the Commonwealth and other states and nations related to trade and commerce; support of Massachusetts goods and services in national and international markets.

House Chair: Rep. [Angelo J. Puppolo, Jr.](https://malegislature.gov/Legislators/Profile/AJP1/192/Members)

House Vice Chair: Rep. [Kathleen R. LaNatra](https://malegislature.gov/Legislators/Profile/KPL1/192/Members)

## [Joint Committee on Public Service](https://malegislature.gov/Committees/Detail/J23/192)

The Joint Committee on Public Service considers matters concerning the salaries, civil service and retirement of public employees (including the retirement of judges, court personnel and county employees but excluding the salaries of said judges, court personnel and county employees), collective bargaining for state employees and other public employees and such other matters as may be referred.

House Chair: Rep. [Kenneth I. Gordon](https://malegislature.gov/Legislators/Profile/KIG1/192/Members)

House Vice Chair: Rep. [Natalie M. Higgins](https://malegislature.gov/Legislators/Profile/N_H1/192/Members)

[Bills under consideration](https://malegislature.gov/Committees/Detail/J23/192/Bills)

## [Joint Committee on Revenue](https://malegislature.gov/Committees/Detail/J26/192)

The Joint Committee on Revenue considers matters concerning Federal financial assistance, state fees, the raising of revenue for the Commonwealth by means of taxation, and certain matters relating to the local property tax.

House Chair: Rep. [Mark J. Cusack](https://malegislature.gov/Legislators/Profile/MJC1/192/Members)

House Vice Chair: Rep. [David Paul Linsky](https://malegislature.gov/Legislators/Profile/DPL1/192/Members)
